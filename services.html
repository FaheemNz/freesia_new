HVAC

At Gardinia, our team of experienced civil engineers are responsible for the construction and maintenance of building projects. The civil service includes structural, masonry and finishes works and joinery.

From planning, design and budgeting to surveying and construction management and analysis, civil engineering covers a broad range of important elements in the construction process.

The team at Gardinia are dynamic and forward-thinking and always take modern day environmental concerns into consideration. Wherever possible, we help to address issues of pollution and consider the potential of sustainable energy within any given project.

MEP
Plumbing

At Gardinia, our team are experts in mechanical electrical and plumbing (MEP) for residential, commercial and hotel construction projects in Dubai. We advise clients on the most efficient systems to meet their individual requirements.

From conceptual design and development, planning, coordination and procurement to installation and testing and commissioning, we offer fully-comprehensive MEP services.

We undertake supply, installation and maintenance of all MEP contracts for our clients which includes low, medium and high-rise residential buildings with unlimited storeys.

Our experienced international team bring a wealth of knowledge to the table and work closely with local authorities in Dubai to ensure full compliance with the latest industry regulations to ensure timely authority approvals.
